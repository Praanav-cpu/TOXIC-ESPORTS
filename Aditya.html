    Aditya "ghostaditya" Horo emerged as the best BGMI IGL (In Game Leader) In BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India), an IGL (In-Game Leader) plays a crucial role in leading the team, making strategic decisions, and guiding teammates through the game. The IGL is responsible for: Game Plan Creation: Before the match starts, the IGL formulates a plan, deciding on drop locations, rotations, and tactics based on the map, zone, and enemy movements. Mid-Game Calls: During the match, the IGL makes critical decisions like when to engage, retreat, reposition, or rotate, keeping an eye on the shrinking zone and enemy positions. Team Coordination: Ensures all team members are working together efficiently, maintaining communication, and executing strategies as a unit. Adaptability: An IGL needs to be quick on their feet, making on-the-spot decisions depending on the game’s changing dynamics. Leadership: They lead not just through tactics but by keeping morale high, offering clear communication, and managing stress during intense situations. In competitive BGMI, a good IGL can often be the deciding factor between victory and defeat, as they hold the reins of the team's overall performance.
